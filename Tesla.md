## What a Megapack includes
- Enclosure ships with battery modules, bi‚Äëdirectional inverter, thermal management, AC main breaker, wiring, and protection already installed and tested in‚Äëhouse. [impulsoragdl](https://impulsoragdl.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/Ficha-Tecnica-Mega-Pack.pdf)
- It functions as a **turnkey** AC‚Äëcoupled unit, so no DC wiring is required onsite between batteries and inverter. [impulsoragdl](https://impulsoragdl.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/Ficha-Tecnica-Mega-Pack.pdf)

## Site and civil requirements
- The EPC or installer must prepare foundations and provide seismic anchoring per Tesla‚Äôs Megapack System Installation Manual and local structural/seismic codes. [greenburghny](https://www.greenburghny.com/DocumentCenter/View/15305/Commissioning-Plan)
- Site work typically includes grading, fenced equipment yard, drainage, access roads, and clear working space and egress around each unit per fire code.

## Electrical and communications
- Once set, Megapack requires connection of AC conductors to the site transformer or switchgear and a communications cable for SCADA/controls. [docs.planning.org](https://docs.planning.org.uk/20210319/202/QPV6H7SOJ8900/zisuxgvois9byi3q.pdf)
- Supported protocols include Modbus TCP, DNP3, and REST API for integration with plant controllers and utility interfaces. [impulsoragdl](https://impulsoragdl.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/Ficha-Tecnica-Mega-Pack.pdf)

## Commissioning workflow
- Construction contractor installs, anchors, and wires the Megapack system and tracks progress on a Tesla construction checklist app. [greenburghny](https://www.greenburghny.com/DocumentCenter/View/15305/Commissioning-Plan)
- After the construction checklist and photos are submitted and approved, Tesla performs remote or onsite commissioning, including validation tests and bringing packs to roughly 80% state of charge, which can take 24‚Äì48 hours. [greenburghny](https://www.greenburghny.com/DocumentCenter/View/15305/Commissioning-Plan)

## Storage, warranty, and shutdown
- If units will sit more than about 12 months between manufacture and installation, the site manager must coordinate with Tesla to avoid permanent damage and warranty issues. [greenburghny](https://www.greenburghny.com/DocumentCenter/View/15305/Commissioning-Plan)
